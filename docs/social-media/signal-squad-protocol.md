# Signal Squad — First-Hour Engagement Protocol

**Purpose**: Ensure every LeadHunterIQ social post gets engagement velocity in the first 60 minutes.
LinkedIn's algorithm weights first-hour engagement heavily — 10+ comments in the first hour = 5-10x reach.

**Governing Order**: CMO — Algorithm Optimization (Gap M3)

---

## What Is the Signal Squad?

A group of 5-10 people who commit to engaging with LeadHunterIQ posts within 30 minutes of publishing. This is NOT fake engagement — every comment must be substantive and authentic.

## Squad Members (Target: 8-10)

| # | Category | Target |
|---|----------|--------|
| 1-2 | Talpro India team members | Internal |
| 3-4 | Friendly founders (SaaS/staffing) | Network |
| 5-6 | Industry contacts (HR/staffing leaders) | Clients/prospects |
| 7-8 | LinkedIn connections (active commenters) | Community |

**Recruitment pitch**: "I'm launching a new product for IT staffing. Would you be willing to engage with 2-3 posts/week on LinkedIn? I'll do the same for your content."

## How It Works

### Publishing Day Protocol

1. **Bhaskar publishes** the post on LinkedIn (company page or personal profile)
2. **Within 5 minutes**: Share the post link in the Signal Squad WhatsApp group
3. **Message format**: "New post live! Topic: [1-line summary]. Link: [URL]"
4. **Squad members engage within 30 minutes**:
   - Leave a substantive comment (2+ sentences, not "great post")
   - Like/react to the post
   - Like other squad members' comments (creates engagement cascade)
5. **Bhaskar replies** to every comment within 60 minutes (drives comment count higher)

### Comment Quality Rules

**Good comments** (substantive, adds value):
- "This resonates — we saw something similar with [personal example]. The timing aspect is especially true for..."
- "Interesting data point about GCC expansions. In our experience, [relevant industry observation]..."
- "Have you considered [thoughtful question]? I've been thinking about this in the context of..."

**Bad comments** (do NOT post these):
- "Great post!" / "Well said!" / "Agree!" / Fire emoji only
- Copy-paste comments
- Irrelevant sales pitches
- Comments shorter than 10 words

### Cross-Promotion Protocol

| Action | Who | Frequency |
|--------|-----|-----------|
| Bhaskar comments on company page posts from personal profile | Bhaskar | Every company page post |
| Company page reshares Bhaskar's best personal posts | Admin | 1x/week |
| Talpro India page shares LeadHunterIQ launch posts | Talpro admin | Launch week, then 1x/month |
| Squad members reshare 1 post/week | All squad | Weekly |

## Posting Schedule

Optimal LinkedIn posting times for Indian B2B audience:
- **Tuesday-Thursday**: 8:00-9:00 AM IST (commute scroll)
- **Monday**: 10:00-11:00 AM IST (settled into work)
- **Friday**: 12:00-1:00 PM IST (lunch scroll)
- **Avoid**: Weekends, holidays, after 6 PM

Post the WhatsApp alert **immediately** after publishing — the 30-minute window starts now.

## Tracking

Weekly metrics to track:
- Average comments in first 60 minutes
- Average impressions per post
- Squad participation rate (% of squad who engaged)
- Best performing post (topic, time, comment count)
