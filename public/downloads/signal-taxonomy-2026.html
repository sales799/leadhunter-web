Introduction

This taxonomy classifies every business signal that indicates upcoming IT staffing demand in India. Built from 15+ years of staffing industry expertise, it covers 45+ signal types organized into 7 categories.

Each signal type includes: what it is, where to find it, typical lead time before hiring begins, and how to act on it.

How to use this guide: Focus on the signal categories most relevant to your agency's verticals. Start monitoring 2-3 signal types, then expand as your capacity grows. The "Lead Time" column tells you how far ahead of hiring demand each signal typically appears.

7. Signal Scoring Overview

Not all signals are created equal. Use these criteria to prioritize which signals to act on first:

Scoring Dimensions

DimensionWeightDescription
Company sizeHighFortune 500 / large enterprise = higher staffing volume
Signal freshnessHighRecent signals (< 7 days) have highest conversion rate
Signal stageHighEarly-stage signals = less competition, higher relationship value
Geography matchMediumSignal location matching your agency's delivery capability
Technology matchMediumHiring for tech stacks your agency specializes in
Multi-signal corroborationHighMultiple signal types from the same company = high confidence
Budget indicatorsMediumFunding amount, company revenue, office size as budget proxy
Pro tip: The highest-value signals are early-stage, multi-corroborated, and from large companies. A Fortune 500 registering a new Indian entity (MCA filing) + signing an office lease + hiring a country head = ultra-high confidence signal worth immediate outreach.
